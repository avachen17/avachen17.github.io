Introduction

MangaCulture

MangaCulture is a digital magazine that showcases trending Japanese manga, otherwise known as comics or graphic novels. We focus on the artist and the origin stories of some of the most popular mangas in the industry. We also love to share drawing and manga art tips and techniques. As a result, MangaCulture is an essential resource for aspiring artists.
