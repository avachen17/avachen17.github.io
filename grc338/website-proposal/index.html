Introduction

Bunny Stationery

My fictitious shop is called Bunny Stationery shop. The company is in the stationery/paper business. We sell paper, memo pads, stickers, washi tape, pens, brush pens, paints, journals, and other supplies.

Target audience

My target audience are people who love to bullet journal and scrapbook. They have an appreciation for color and creative designs.

Their primary goal is to buy more supplies for scrapbooking, and they like to express themselves in a creative way. Therefore, they would be visiting my shop website to buy more stationery products.

About

[Photo of the owner's office.]

Bunny Stationer was founded in 2010 by Ava, a longtime scrapbooker and journaller! She is passionate about stationery and is always looking for unique items to add to her collection. She started Bunny Stationery to offer high quality stationery items at a fair price. Through her business, she hopes to encourage people to start journalling and become passionate about stationery.
